What are the early symptoms of diabetes?
Early symptoms of diabetes can include:

• Increased thirst and frequent urination
• Extreme hunger
• Unexplained weight loss
• Fatigue and irritability
• Blurred vision
• Slow-healing sores or frequent infections
• Tingling or numbness in hands or feet (for type 2 diabetes)

Type 1 diabetes symptoms often develop quickly, while type 2 diabetes symptoms may develop gradually or even be absent. If you're experiencing these symptoms, it's important to consult with a healthcare provider for proper diagnosis and treatment.
