About

Making Augmented Healthcare Intelligence Accessible.

  • 2021-2024

    Ideation and Team Formation

    We found each other in hackathons and formed a relationship forged by common pain points experienced in the healthcare. We listened to other patients and doctors from the Port Hackathon 2021 at CERN, the Innosuisse Business Concept (2021) where we won the Best Life Sciences Award.

  • December 2024-May 2025

    Prototyping and Tests

    Creating a prototype for a chatbot that can help patients with their healthcare needs and an open-source platform for healthcare providers to facilitate administrative tasks.

  • May 2025 to June 2025

    Soft Launch

    We are launching the first version for early access and testing.

  • July 2025

    Launch and Scaling plans

    We are looking for partners to scale our product and reach more patients.
